K-12

For every individual step along the journey.

An education should be as individual as each child

The Clevara platform supports an entire learning community of students, teachers, parents, and administrators with solutions for guiding every learner’s critical early steps.

In the early years, it can be hard to track the progress of each child as their own learning strengths come to the fore. And as that learning grows, so does the complexity of education. Using our early stage benchmarking, we can identify a child strengths and help them through the areas where they need the most help.
